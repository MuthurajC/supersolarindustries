Services/technical skills

Roof Top
Rooftop solar, also known as rooftop PV, is a type of solar system that is installed on the roofs of buildings, such as homes, offices, schools, or factories.
Solar park
A solar park is a large-scale installation of solar photovoltaic (PV) panels that generate electricity from sunlight. Solar parks can range from a few megawatts (MW) to several gigawatts (GW) of capacity, depending on the size and location of the land.
Micro plant
Micro plants, also known as micro solar power plants or micro solar generators, are small-scale solar systems.
